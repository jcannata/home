who we are

Cannata + Associates, Inc. is a software engineering firm located in San Diego, California. Founded in 2003 and built upon 17 years creating mission critical test and measurement software, we provide software development solutions encompassing a broad field of technologies including machine control, data acquisition and analysis, as well as end user applications.

what we do

Working closely with our clients we provide the software skills to bring products to market quickly. Our developers follow current best practices and processes with an emphasis on solutions based on Microsoft .NET technologies.

We have built several end to end applications for a variety of customers. From automatic produce labeling to an FDA approved orthopaedic drill.

All of our developers have degrees in Electrical Engineering and have extensive experience in interfacing with both custom and off the shelf hardware when needed. We have extensive experience with an extensive array of interfaces:

  • BLE 4+
  • USB
  • ModBus
  • I2C
  • SPI
